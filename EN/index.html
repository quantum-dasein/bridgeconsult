1 // ABOUT THE COMPANY

About Us

BRIDGE CONSULT is an independent consulting firm specializing in contract engineering, construction contract administration, and dispute resolution in international infrastructure projects.

The company provides expert support to public sector clients, international financial institutions (IFIs), investors, and contractors in the execution of large-scale infrastructure projects, including those financed by multilateral development banks.

The company was founded in 2016 and is registered in the Republic of Uzbekistan.

Mission

The mission of Bridge Consult is to support the successful implementation of international infrastructure projects through professional contract engineering, effective construction contract management, and the application of modern dispute resolution mechanisms.

We strive to ensure transparency, legal certainty, and effective risk management at all stages of the project lifecycle by integrating international best practices, FIDIC standards, and deep expertise in contract and project management.

Our goal is to establish a reliable legal and managerial foundation for cooperation between the public sector, IFIs, investors, and contractors.

2 // SERVICES OVERVIEW

Services Overview

Bridge Consult provides consulting services for international infrastructure projects.

Contract Engineering (FIDIC / EPC)

  • Structuring, Documentation & Tenders: Preparation of a complete documentation package for procurement procedures, including technical specifications and evaluation criteria.
  • Tender Engineering: Bid analysis, identifying hidden risks in tender documentation.
  • Contract Drafting: Drafting and adapting international construction contracts (FIDIC and EPC).
  • Contract Strategy Audit: Evaluating and selecting the optimal contract model.

Construction Contract Administration

  • Claims Management: Professional analysis, preparation, and defense of the client's position; preventive measures to avoid disputes.
  • Risk & Change Management: Risk identification, regulation of all modifications in scope, time, and cost.
  • Budget & Quality Control: Budget, procurement, and quality control in accordance with technical and regulatory requirements.

Dispute Resolution and Arbitration

  • Dispute Avoidance Boards (DAAB/DAB): Representation of parties, consulting support, and provision of experts.
  • Mediation: Applying mediation procedures to achieve out-of-court settlements.
  • Arbitration Support: Expert support and preparation of evidence in Claims & Arbitration procedures.

Analytical Expertise and Training

  • Financial Risk Assessment: Forensic Delay Analysis and Quantum Calculation (variation and damage calculations).
  • Local Adaptation: Aligning international contract conditions with the legislation of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
  • Institutional Development: Conducting seminars and training on effective contract administration and claims management.
Innovative Approach

Top-Tier Standards

Integrating world-class dispute resolution practices into real-world project conditions.

The International Federation of Consulting Engineers (FIDIC) provides the gold standard for standard forms of contract. We possess expertise in applying the full suite of forms, including:

Red BookConditions of Contract for Construction
Yellow BookConditions of Contract for Plant and Design-Build
Silver BookConditions of Contract for EPC/Turnkey Projects
Pink BookMDB Harmonised Edition
Applied for retrospective analysis of construction delays. We utilize the Society of Construction Law (SCL) Protocol methods, including Time Impact Analysis and Time Slice Analysis, to prove or disprove a contractor's entitlement to an Extension of Time (EOT) and cost compensation.
A pre-arbitral dispute resolution mechanism. We assist in structuring DAAB operations, preparing Position Papers, and representing clients at Board hearings, thereby minimizing the risks of escalating the case to costly international arbitration.
